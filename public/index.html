Common Questions

Everything you need
to know about FlowHer

Is FlowHer only for people with a formal ADHD diagnosis? →
Not at all. FlowHer is for any woman who has ever felt like she was working twice as hard just to keep up — whether you have a diagnosis, are in the process of getting one, or simply know that the standard tools have never worked for your brain. You belong here.
What makes FlowHer different from other productivity apps? →
Most productivity apps were built for neurotypical brains. They assume you can just start a task, follow a schedule, and stay focused. FlowHer was built for brains that do not work that way. Every feature — from the AI-powered Smallest Step Engine to the RSD Toolkit — was designed around how neurodivergent brains actually function, not how productivity influencers say they should.
What is the founding member rate and how does it work? →
The first 200 women to join FlowHer Core pay $24.99/month — and that rate is locked forever, for as long as you stay subscribed. After 200 spots are filled, regular pricing moves to $39.99/month. Founding members also get first access to every new feature as we build.
Can I cancel anytime? →
Yes — no contracts, no questions asked. You can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel and want to come back, you will rejoin at the regular rate, not your founding rate, so most members choose to stay.
What does the free plan include? →
The free plan includes daily mood check-in, 3 affirmations per day, a basic focus timer, and 5 brain dumps per month. It is a real plan — not a trial. You can use it as long as you like. Core unlocks the AI-powered tools, unlimited features, and the full workplace survival toolkit.
Is my data private and secure? →
Yes. Your data is stored securely through Supabase and is never sold to third parties. The Unmask Space and Win Journal are private to you. We take your privacy seriously — especially because we know how personal this information is.
